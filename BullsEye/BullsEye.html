★ Bull's Eye ★

Welcome to the awesome game of Bull's Eye where you can compete with friends by winning points by dragging the slider to the correct number.

Your goal it to place the slider as close to as possible to the target number. The closer you are, the more points you score. You can even get bonus points!

Scoring:
Your number subtracted from the target number (max 100pts)

Bonus:
+100 for hitting it exactly
+50 for being within 5
-20 for being more than 20 away
